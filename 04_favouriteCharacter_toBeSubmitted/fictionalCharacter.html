Brewster

ABOUT

Brewster (or マスター "Master" in Japanese) is a character who appears in the world of the Animal Crossing games. He is a pigeon that runs a café called "The Roost" and has been well liked by the fanbase since his first appearance in 2005.
Nintendo has announced that he will appear in Animal Crossing: New Horizon starting in fall 2021.
